A Charming Seaside Street

Badaguan

Badaguan integrates the park with the courtyard. There are lush trees everywhere, flowers in full bloom in four seasons, and different varieties of street trees in ten roads.

Representative buildings in Qingdao

Trestle

It is one of the scenic spots in Qingdao seashore scenic area. There is a two-story octagonal building in the form of nationality, called "Huilan Pavilion". Tourists stand beside the pavilion and enjoy the huge waves coming from all levels. "Feige Huilan" is known as one of the "ten scenic spots in Qingdao".

The square named by the movement

May Fourth Square

The name comes from the May 4th student movement in China in 1919. The sculpture is made of steel plates, supplemented by a fiery red outer coating. Its shape adopts a spiral steel plate structure combination. With the practice of washing, simple lines and thick texture, it shows the image of "strong wind" rising in the air, giving people a "force" shock.
